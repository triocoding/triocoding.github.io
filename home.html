What are vedas?

The Vedas are a large body of religious texts originating in ancient India.
They are considered to be one of the most sacred scriptures of the hindu religion.They are claimed to be amoung the world's oldest scriptures.In short,the Vedas, “knowledge,” are the oldest texts of Hinduism

What are the moral values in the vedas?

The morals values depicted in the vedas are:-
1.Satya
2.Dharma
3.Shanthi
4.Prema
5.Ahimsa
6.Brahmacharya
