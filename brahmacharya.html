Brahmacharya

Brahmacharya is the practice of non-excess, moderation, and right use of energy. It's the practice of experiencing the pleasure, wonder, and the mystery of life without over-indulgence.

One day gandhiji said about being a brahmacharya to a question "Why did you take up Brahmacharya?".He said that he took up brahmacharya to free himself from the physical world.He wanted to have complete control of his body and mind.He had to completely detach himself from all relationships and ties with this world.
Benefits of Brahmacharya
1.enhance and raise our energy and physical levels.
2. complete consciousness and energy in our being
3.control of body and mind
